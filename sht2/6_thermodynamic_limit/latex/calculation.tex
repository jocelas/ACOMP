g(r) = 1 + \frac{1}{2\pi^2\rho r}\times\int _0 ^\infty {\rm d}k\ k \sin(kr)[S(k) - 1]
\]

Because we cannot calculate the integral to infinity, we will use a
cutoff (The standard cutoff is \(20/\sigma\)): \texttt{cutoff}

To discretize \(k\), we will use a \texttt{kStep}:

\texttt{np.arange(0,\ cutoff,\ kStep)}
